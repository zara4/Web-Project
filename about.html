How we started.

Naiki Organization started solely by Dr Muhammad Habib, inorder to promote concept of wall of kindness digitally, naiki was introduced so that people may donate their old clothes, accessories and etc to needy people instead of throwing them away.Gradually, the concept involved into providing food for poor and shelter for homeless, evolving into a digital platform for people to donate locally.
